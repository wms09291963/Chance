Will's Game of Chance

From 1 to 5 Players

Tired of the House winning all the time? Will's Game of Chance (from 1 to 5 Players) is truly a game of chance with no favoritism for the House. Each Player starts with a wallet of $1,000. Players place bets which are then reflected in the pot, and sumulteneously roll a pair of dice. Whoever has the highest combination of numbers wins the round. In the event of a tie the pot is split between the winners. Whoever wins (has the most in their wallet) at the end of 5 rounds wins. If 1 Player only will play against the House. Each Player starts with $1,000 in their wallet (including the House). Each Player places bet. If House is playing a random number generated between 1 and the total of their wallet for house bet. Each Player Rolls the dice [produces Random number between 2 and 12].
