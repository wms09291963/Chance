Will's Game of Chance

For 2 Players

Tired of the House winning all the time? Will's Game of Chance (for 2 Players) is truly a game of chance with no favoritism. Each Player starts with a wallet of $1,000. Players choose the number of rounds they wish to play, place bets for each round, and simultaneously roll a pair of dice. The player with the highest roll wins the round. The player with the most winnings at the end of the designated number of rounds wins the game. and sumulteneously roll a pair of dice. Whoever has the highest combination of numbers wins the round. In the event of a tie the pot is split between the winners.
